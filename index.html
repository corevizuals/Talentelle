Empowering Women Entrepreneurs Through Stories

What is Talentelle?
Talentelle is an initiative dedicated to empowering women entrepreneurs by providing them with the tools, resources, and support needed to grow their businesses. It focuses on creating a collaborative environment where women can build their skills, gain confidence, and connect with like-minded individuals. Through workshops, mentorship, and networking opportunities, Talentelle helps women navigate challenges and achieve success in their entrepreneurial journeys.

How She Dares

Women Entrepreneurship Movie of the Year

How She Dares is an inspiring documentary following Zoonie, a woman entrepreneur, as she explores her identity and the courage of bold women worldwide. From Montreal to Vietnam, it showcases stories of women breaking barriers and encourages others to take brave steps in their lives and businesses.
